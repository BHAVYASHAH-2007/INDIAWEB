The Battle of Buxar was fought between 22 and 23 October 1764, between the forces of the British East India Company, under the command of Major Hector Munro, against the combined armies of Balwant Singh, Maharaja of the Benaras State; Mir Qasim, the Nawab of Bengal; Shuja-ud-daula, the Nawab of Awadh; and Shah Alam II, the Emperor of the Mughal Empire.
